Our Advantages

Sales Increase

With the help of the network Artour sets up in the local market, any hotel that partners with us enjoys greater exposure for the products it sells, whilst every vendor gains greater access to that many more hotels and consequently, clients.

User Experience

Customers enjoy a more efficient, high-end service at the hotel reception, whilst reception staff have more tools and accessible information at their disposal, allowing them to take the customer through the product on offer and avoid any and all misunderstandings.

Cloud-based System

With an automated booking system and full vendor integration, we have left behind all the old ways of selling visitors accompanying services, replacing them with a one-click, completed transaction where all parties are kept updated in real-time.

Streamlining the operation

The system cuts booking time, confirming the booking instantly, therefore eliminating any need for confirmation emails or phone calls.

A simple interface

The system interface allows users to master it in very little time, and with no special skills required.

Administrative Software

The system enables tracking employee activities & generating comprehensive reports. The system will learn the quality of service on the go and will know how to recognise trends, and alert to shifts in customer and user satisfaction, thereby making the hospitality field a one-stop-shop operation without any loose ends.

Our Story

Artour is the brainchild of a group of hoteliers who had realised that external hotel services available to guests at reception is an area that could be better-managed and made more efficient in a way that allows the guest to access all services available to them via the hotel receptionist, whilst simultaneously enabling the hotel manager to turn their hotel into a one-stop-shop for any and all other services it offers.
